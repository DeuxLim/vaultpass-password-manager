Support

If you need help with your VaultPass account, use the contact method provided by your VaultPass operator.

Common issues

  • Can’t sign in: double-check email/password, then try again. If you recently enabled 2FA, make sure your device time is correct.
  • Account temporarily locked: wait until the retry timer ends, then try again.
  • Lost 2FA device: use recovery codes if you saved them. Otherwise, contact support for account recovery.
